Catering Management Software

Quote the event, hold the date, take the deposit, cost every tray. Culvana, built by the team behind Recipe Costing Software, runs your catering back office so you can run the event.

Grow Your Catering Business With Catering Food Cost Software

For catering operations, great food and great service win the repeat business. But the margin is decided in the back office: recipe costing, inventory, purchasing and quotes. Most caterers don't have spare hours for that paperwork.

Culvana handles the back end automatically. The AI reads your invoices the day they land, updates ingredient costs, applies yields, and reprices every recipe. The quote you send this afternoon is built on today's costs, not last quarter's.

Build Recipes and See Menu Item Cost Breakdowns

Cost every tray, platter and per-head package on facts:

  • Create recipes and sub-recipes for your entire menu.
  • View total recipe costs including items, materials and labor.
  • Scale recipes to see cost by serving size or percentage, from a 10-top lunch to a 400-cover wedding.

Track Your Inventory in Real Time

Ingredient stock counts, purchasing trends, food costs and vendor prices, live:

  • Calculate food costs for any period or event.
  • See current product price, physical count and value.
  • Get alerts when inventory hits its minimum par level.
  • Track inventory by day or shift to control food cost impact.

Simplify and Automate Order Purchasing

Order exactly what the booked events need. No more, no less:

  • Place orders based on historical trends and par levels.
  • See suggested purchases based on machine learning.
  • Get real-time alerts to buy ingredients at the right time.
  • Review previous transactions for bookkeeping.

Shopping Lists Built From Recipes and Orders

Culvana generates shopping lists automatically from your scaled recipes, menu items and previous orders. Book a 250-head event, scale the menu, and the shopping list writes itself with the exact quantities to hit par without waste.

Quotes, Deposits and the Books

Quote from a brief, hold the date, take the deposit. The deposit sits correctly in deferred revenue until the food is actually served. Because Culvana includes the books, catering revenue, food cost and event profitability live in one system instead of three.

Why Caterers Choose Culvana

  • Reduce costs: price creep gets caught the day it lands, not after the event margin is gone.
  • Save time: invoices, counts, orders and journal entries stop being manual work.
  • Serve customers better: accurate pricing, allergen declarations and prep instructions on every recipe.
  • No hardware, no paper: runs in a browser on the devices you already own. $199 a location with 2,000 Culvana AI Credits included every month.
