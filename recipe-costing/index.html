Recipe & menu costing

Recipe and Menu Costing Software

Stop guessing what your recipes cost. Culvana, built by the team behind Recipe Costing Software, builds recipes and sub-recipes, tracks yields and labor, flags allergens, and reprices your whole menu the day a vendor price changes.

Easily Manage Your Recipes and Costs

Culvana's recipe costing software gives you the cost breakdown for every menu item, covering items, materials and labor, with a total food cost in your currency and a food cost percentage. Knowing your recipe costs is how you make the strategic decisions that reduce expenses and improve your bottom line.

Unlike a standalone costing tool, Culvana is one platform: the AI reads your invoices, updates ingredient costs, moves inventory, and recalculates every affected recipe automatically. Nobody in your building does data entry.

Create Recipes and Add Sub-Recipes Easily

Build recipes and sub-recipes as deep as your menu goes. The mother dough inside the loaf inside the sandwich, costed all the way down. Every recipe shows total cost including items, materials and the labor to make it.

Do You Know Your Recipe Costs?

Scale any recipe by percentage or serving size and Culvana recalculates the cost instantly. Let the software do the math so you know the cost of a recipe at any portion size, then check what that's worth to you in the savings calculator.

Identify Costly Food Items and Materials

Enter quantity and portion size for any ingredient and Culvana calculates cost based on the weight selected, metric or imperial, with yield applied at the supplier, ingredient and recipe level. You'll see exactly which items drive the cost of each dish, so you can price the menu or rework the recipe on facts.

Add Labor and Cost Out Menu Items

Add theoretical labor to recipes to calculate menu costs accurately. Food, materials, labor and overhead stay separated for analysis, so you can zero in on true food cost without the noise.

Write Up Preparations and Procedures

Don't leave your cooks guessing. Type directions into each recipe with formatting, highlights and links to photos or videos, so every plate comes out the same, on every shift, at every location.

Identify Allergens With Every Recipe

Culvana identifies and displays allergens as recipes and menu items are created. Your kitchen knows where cross-contamination risk lives, and your menu can declare allergens so guests with food allergies can order safely.

Scale Recipes by Percentage or Portions

Cooks can scale recipes from the recipe page or the recipe report, on a phone or tablet, and every ingredient stays in proportion. Accurate at any batch size, from a single plate to a banquet run.
